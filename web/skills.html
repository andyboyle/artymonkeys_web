Core Skills How Can Arty Monkeys Help?
Cognitive
  • Problem solving
  • Fine motor skills
  • Spatial awareness
  • Memory
  • Through use of art and craft materials and tools children will develop their fine motor skills.
  • By choosing suitable tools and materials problem solving skills will be developed.
  • Children will be discussing stories, rhymes and illustrations from books which will improve their memory and observation skills.
Language Acquisition
  • Speech
  • Communication
  • Phonological awareness
  • Through stories, songs and rhymes to introduce and close each class children will begin to recognise rhyme and initial sounds. These will be discussed and activities will be designed around these themes.
Social
  • Self-esteem
  • Sharing
  • Respect
  • Emotional self-regulation
  • Through following class routines and instructions the children will learn about successful sharing of equipment and space.
  • By regularly sharing their own artwork with the group and celebrating their masterpieces on our website and Facebook.
