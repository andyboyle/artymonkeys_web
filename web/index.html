What We Do

Our Mission is to create a relaxed and supportive environment which ignites the imagination and creativity of young minds.

Through exploring different materials such as paint, clay, sponges, rollers and textured papers we will help develop your child's cognitive, social and language skills.
Our weekly classes are all based on popular stories or nursery rhymes which gives children a context for their ideas and creativity.
